The Muscular System

Welcome to the Muscular System. Today you' ll be taking a tour around the muscle system and you will be learning the basic functions of the Muscles and how they work when you're active and inactive. Muscles are the parts of your body that make you move, climbing over rocks or running down the street. Muscles are simple yet complex and they come in three main tissue types which are Visceral, Cardiac, and Skeletal muscle. Together the Muscular system works with the other body systems in order to complete the human body.
