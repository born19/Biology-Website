Excretory System

The excretory system filters blood and gets rid of waste that your body has. This consists of your kidneys, liver, bladder, and your urethra. The kidney's filter blood, making sure there is no harmful chemicals, while your urethra and bladder will store and get rid of urine. If you want to learn more about this body system, URINE luck.
