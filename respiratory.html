The Respiratory System

There is nothing more important to the body than air. Wouldn't you like to gain information on how your body uses and obtains air. Well, come visit the respiratory sysytem to find out. You will see how the lungs contract in order to spread oxygen throughtout the capillaries and refresh the whole bodywith oxygen. See how far air travels from your nasal cavity down your trachea. The respiratory system works in such amazing ways, that it will take your breath away.
